Core Principles of AI Governance

A framework for responsible AI development and deployment

Transparency

AI systems should be understandable, with clear documentation of their capabilities, limitations, and decision-making processes.

Accountability

Clear lines of responsibility must be established for AI systems, ensuring organizations and individuals are answerable for outcomes.

Fairness

AI systems must be designed to avoid discrimination and ensure equitable treatment across diverse populations and use cases.

Privacy

AI development and deployment must respect individual privacy rights and protect sensitive personal information.

Safety

AI systems should be robust, secure, and designed to minimize risks to individuals, organizations, and society.

Collaboration

Effective AI governance requires cooperation among stakeholders including policymakers, researchers, industry, and civil society.
