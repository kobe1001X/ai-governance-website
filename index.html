Core Principles of AI Governance

Safety First

Fairness & Equity

AI must be developed and deployed in ways that promote fairness, reduce bias, and ensure equitable outcomes for all members of society.

Transparency

About This Initiative

AI Governance is an independent research initiative dedicated to exploring the ethical, legal, and policy dimensions of artificial intelligence. We bridge the gap between technical AI development and societal impact, working to ensure that AI systems are developed and deployed in ways that benefit humanity.

Our work combines rigorous academic research with practical policy analysis, bringing together perspectives from computer science, philosophy, law, and the social sciences.
